Welcome to Coffee Cave

Despite it being called a ‘bean’, coffee is a fruit. The ‘beans’ grow on a bush and are found in the centre of a berry, known as a coffee cherry.

It’s widely known that Brazil is one of the biggest coffee hotspots of the world, but did you know it’s responsible for around a third of all the coffee in the world? Brazil is the number one coffee producer, followed by Vietnam and Colombia.

Before coffee was discovered to be a delicious beverage it was a food. East African tribes would grind the berries then mix them with animal fat and eat them..

Our Products

Espresso

Espresso is a delicious concentrated form of coffee, served in shots and it’s often the coffee base of many other beverages and it's usually served in small demitasse-style cups for this reason.

Cappucino

A cappuccino is the perfect balance of espresso, steamed milk and foam. This coffee is all about the structure and the even splitting of all elements into equal thirds.

Latte

A caffè latte is a milk coffee that has a silky layer of foam to highlight the drink. A latte will be made up of two shots of espresso, steamed milk and a final layer of frothed milk on top.
